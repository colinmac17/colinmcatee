## 2020
To those who are miraculously reading this, hello there 👋. If you didn't initially catch the Star Wars reference we can still be friends, but do know I am disappointed.

![Hello there](https://i.redd.it/qj2fbx8tudi31.jpg "Hello There. General Kenobi.")

We are currently in the midst of a very terrible and peculiar year. A year which has given many of us more time than ever to work on new projects. I spent the first half of 2020 building and shipping the first version of [Productive Recruit](https://productiverecruit.com). Now I am in the midst of growing the business with marketing and sales. Additionally, I am planning a new B2B SaaS product - something that I have been wanting to do for some time. I am amazed that I have been able to do all this while working full time and pursuing my masters in Computer Science.

## Why am I here?
Why not start writing I thought? Why not start a blog? As an introverted/insecure person, it is scary for me to put myself out there. This is especially true for things related to my career and journey becoming a founder. 

I am a former college athlete. My entire life up until age 22 felt very tied to athletics. Since then, I have learned to code, become a software engineer, started a masters in computer science, and launched my own software products.

## A new brand

While I am the same person at heart, my new brand is much different than before. I used to identify as an athlete, now I identify as a software engineer and founder. This transition was hard, but I've never been happier. I am doing what I love and working towards my goal of financial freedom through my own business. I'm happy I'm here, and I'm ready for the challenges that lie ahead.
